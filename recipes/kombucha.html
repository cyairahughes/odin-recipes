Kombucha

Description

Kombucha is a fermented beverage. I do not know where it originated, but it is really good!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Black Tea
  • White Sugar
  • Scoby

Steps

  1. Boil Black Tea for 20 Minutes
  2. Let Tea Cool Until Room Temp
  3. Add Sugar
  4. Add Scoby
  5. Cover With Towel
  6. Store in Dark Place
  7. Wait 7 Days
  8. Enjoy!
